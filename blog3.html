Defensor is UP Gawad Oblation Awardee

  • PICAO
  • Tue, 01/03/2023

The University of the Philippines named Iloilo Gov. Arthur Defensor Jr. as a recipient of the 2nd UP Gawad Oblation in recognition of his extraordinary service and contribution to the university.

The UP Gawad Oblation is the “highest honour UP can bestow upon its alumni and friends” and is given by the Office of the UP President.

UP President Danilo L. Concepcion said extraordinary service or contribution might come in the form of exceptional teaching, research and public service, creative production, efficient public and university administration, remarkable generosity and voluntarism, and fundraising, among others.

Concepcion also thanked Defensor for all the support that he has given UP during his term as president.

A UP alumnus, Defensor earned his Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy degree from the University of the Philippines Diliman. Under his leadership, the province of Iloilo recently received the Seal of Good Local Governance (SGLG) from the Department of the Interior and Local Government.

MoreProgRes Iloilo is anchored on the following development goals: to increase investment and employment opportunities; to attain food security; to provide accessible health and social services; to protect and manage the resources and assets of the province; to provide effective, efficient and transparent government administrative services; and to maintain order and public safety.

The 2nd UP Gawad Oblation Awarding Ceremonies will be held on Tuesday, January 17, 2023, at 4 p.m. at Ang Bahay ng Alumni in UP Diliman.
